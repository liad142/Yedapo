## 6. FAQ (Objection Killers)

**Q: Is the free plan really free forever?**
A: Yes. No credit card. No trial that expires. You get 3 AI summaries and 5 Ask AI questions every day, indefinitely. We cap usage, not time.

**Q: What happens when I hit the free limit?**
A: Your existing summaries stay accessible. You just cannot generate new ones until the next day. If you consistently hit the ceiling, that is your signal -- the content you consume outgrew the free plan.

**Q: Can I cancel anytime?**
A: One click, no questions. Cancel from your settings page and keep access through the end of your billing period. We do not do exit interviews or dark patterns.
